How it works

Select your favorite TV show or movie cast and upload a photo to see who most closely resembles you! We use face recognition technologies to compare your uploaded face to the ones we made available. The more members a cast contains, the higher the chance of a really similar looking match! More and more TV shows and movies are added periodically, so make sure to revisit us once in a while!
Note: your uploaded photo is deleted immediately after scanning it. We do NOT store this information and never intend to! The results of this process is stored in a cookie on your computer for your convenience, and is deleted automatically after 10 minutes or when your browser is closed.
